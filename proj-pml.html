One example of models studied in this project are Gaussian Processes (GPs). Below is an animated gif to visualise how the posterior of a GP updates as new data points are observed. The GP is used to model a noisy sinusoidal function, and the posterior is updated by computing the covariance kernel matrix. The semi-transparent lines are samples from the GP posterior disrtribution, and the solid line is the mean prediction. The shaded area represents a range of one standard deviation from the mean.
